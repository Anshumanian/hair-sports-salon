# Hair Sport Salon Website - Setup Instructions

## Important: Update These Links

Before launching the website, please update the following links with your actual information:

### 1. Social Media Links (Footer.tsx)
Update these placeholder links in `src/components/Footer.tsx`:
- **Facebook**: Replace `https://www.facebook.com/hairsport` with your actual Facebook page URL
- **Instagram**: Replace `https://www.instagram.com/hairsport` with your actual Instagram handle
- **YouTube**: Replace `https://www.youtube.com/@hairsport` with your actual YouTube channel

### 2. Google Reviews Link (Reviews.tsx)
Update the Google Reviews link in `src/components/Reviews.tsx`:
- Replace `https://g.page/r/YOUR_GOOGLE_PLACE_ID/review` with your actual Google Business review link
- You can get this from your Google Business Profile

### 3. Google Maps Embed (Contact.tsx)
Update the Google Maps iframe in `src/components/Contact.tsx`:
- Replace the placeholder map embed URL with your actual Google Maps embed code
- Get this from Google Maps → Share → Embed a map

### 4. Email Address (Contact.tsx)
Update the email address in `src/components/Contact.tsx`:
- Replace `info@hairsport.com` with your actual business email
